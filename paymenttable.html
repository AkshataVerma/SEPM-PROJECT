SECTION 2 - CANCELLATION POLICY Amor habrjentos continued viability relies on reservations being honoured by our customers, and adequate notice being given of any changes or cancellations.

Accordingly, the following applies: We reserve the right to charge a cancellation fee per cover for any booking that is cancelled, or if numbers are reduced without prior notice. For all bookings, you must notify us of a cancellation or any changes to the number of guests at least 3 hours prior to your booking. For groups over 10 people, including Private Hire & Christmas parties, the required noticed period is 120 hours (5 days). Amor habrjentos reserves the right to charge a cancellation fee of Rs 50.00/- per person for any bookings changed or cancelled within this 3 hour notice period (or 120 hours for groups over 10) The cancellation fee reflects the cost incurred by Amor Habrjentos in terms of staffing, food, and any lost revenue as a result of turning away other potential bookings. We will notify you if any cancellation fee is applied. Any bookings which fail to turn up without any prior notice, will also incur a Rs 70.00/- per person fee. Where possible, the Amor habrjentos team will actively manage restaurant bookings by checking attendance with the main guests/contact we have in our bookings diary.
